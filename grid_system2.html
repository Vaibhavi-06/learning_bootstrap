Agra's Red Fort

After the First Battle of Panipat in 1526, Babur stayed in the fort, in the palace of Ibrahim Lodi. He later built a baoli (step well) in it. His successor, Humayun, was crowned in the fort in 1530. He was defeated at Bilgram in 1540 by Sher Shah Suri.

Jaipur Fort

Amber Fort in Jaipur, a 16th-century architectural marvel, recently hosted US Vice President JD Vance, temporarily closing its doors to tourists for the visit. The fort, with its stunning architecture and vibrant atmosphere, offers a blend of history and adventure for families
