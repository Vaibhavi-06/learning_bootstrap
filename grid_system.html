Fort

Forts, structures built for defense, have a rich history spanning ancient to modern times. They have evolved from simple natural defenses like hills and rivers to complex structures with walls, moats, and bastions. Forts have served not only military purposes but also as residential areas, religious centers, and hubs for trade and administration.
