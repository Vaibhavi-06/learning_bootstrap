Shivneri Fort

About Us

Shivneri is a historic fort near Junnar, renowned as the birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. Surrounded by natural beauty and enriched with cultural heritage, the fort reflects the ideals of bravery, self-rule, and devotion to the motherland. Today, it stands as a proud monument, inspiring visitors and preserving the glorious legacy of the Marathas.

  • Special Features: 7 massive gates, natural water tanks, temple of Goddess Shivai
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March (pleasant weather)
  • Nearby Attractions: Lenyadri Caves, Ozar Ganpati Temple
  • Status: Protected Monument (ASI)

Vision:

To preserve and promote Shivneri as a symbol of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj’s legacy, showcasing its rich history and inspiring values of courage, self-rule, and cultural pride, while developing it as a world-class heritage and eco-tourism destination.

Mission:

To conserve Shivneri Fort with authenticity, provide educational and cultural experiences for visitors, and promote sustainable tourism that honors Shivaji Maharaj’s legacy and the fort’s natural surroundings.

History:

Shivneri Fort located near Junnar in Pune district, is famous as the birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj on 19th February 1630. Built as a strong hill fort with steep slopes and seven gates, it served as a secure place for Jijabai to raise young Shivaji, where he was inspired with the values of Swarajya, courage, and justice. Today, Shivneri stands as a symbol of Maratha pride and a protected heritage site.
